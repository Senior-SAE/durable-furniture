About Us

Welcome to Durable Furniture

At Durable Furniture, we believe that quality and style should go hand in hand. Our mission is to provide you with exceptional furniture pieces that not only enhance the aesthetics of your space but are also built to last.

Our Story

Founded in 1957, Durable Furniture started with a simple idea: to create furniture that stands the test of time. We are passionate about craftsmanship and sustainability, sourcing materials that are both eco-friendly and durable. Our team consists of skilled artisans who pour their expertise into every piece, ensuring that our furniture is not just functional but also a work of art.

Our Values

  • Quality: We prioritize high-quality materials and construction methods to ensure longevity and reliability.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to reducing our environmental impact by using sustainable resources and responsible manufacturing practices.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to provide exceptional service and support to help you find the perfect furniture for your home.

Our Collection

Our diverse range of furniture includes:

  • Living Room: Sofas, coffee tables, and entertainment centers designed for comfort and style.
  • Bedroom: Beds, dressers, and nightstands crafted for a restful retreat.
  • Dining: Tables and chairs that bring family and friends together.
  • Office: Desks and ergonomic chairs that blend functionality with aesthetics.
